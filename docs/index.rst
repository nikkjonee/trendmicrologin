How to Sign In to Your Trend Micro Account

Common Issues and How to Fix Them
________
Some users may face issues when trying to access their Trend Micro sign in account. Here are a few common problems and their solutions:

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): If enabled, make sure to enter the correct verification code sent to your email or phone.
